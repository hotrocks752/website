A SENSORY STEAK EXPERIENCE

CULINARY REVOLUTION

LONDON, N21

HOT ROCKS London, is a fine-dining Steak restaurant located in N21 nestled within the North London Area. Our unique culinary concept seamlessly combines authenticity with innovation to create a truly immersive dining experience.

Explore three floors of culinary delight, on arrival be whisked up to the 6th floor to experience the main restaurant, including live sushi and robata counters adding energy and engagement to your dining experience. Continue the journey up to the 7th-floor rooftop, to the bar, lounge, and terrace area, providing an elevated experience with unparalleled 180-degree views of London Knightsbridge.

Experience the full HOT ROCKS dining experience, and try our Steak menu.

PERFECT HARMONY

CLAP Restaurant made its debut in 2018, opening its doors in the vibrant city of Beirut, Lebanon. Since then, it has rapidly expanded its footprint, establishing itself in key MENA cities such as Dubai, Riyadh, and now London Knightsbridge, nestled within the Knightsbridge Estate.

Founded by the dynamic hospitality group, ADDMIND, CLAP introduces an unparalleled Japanese fine dining concept to the heart of London.

With its exciting atmosphere and exquisite cuisine, CLAP Restaurant has become a renowned destination for food enthusiasts and design aficionados alike.

Private events

For those seeking an elevated and exclusive culinary experience, indulge in the unique and adaptable spaces at CLAP Restaurant. Tables are situated within the main restaurant where diners can still enjoy the atmosphere and ambiance of CLAP.

Our versatile spaces are perfect for hosting a wide range of events, including seated dinners, cocktails and canaps, product displays, press dinners, and more. They are designed to be customized to your requirements, with seasonal menus, expertly paired cocktails, and our special music program and entertainment, ensuring that we exceed your expectations and create unforgettable memories.
